Why you choose Us ?

The emphasis of existing education system, including the training towards JEE (IIT), is highly skewed towards the Quantitative aspect and not much on the Qualitative aspect of Science. Hence, the teaching is based only on the Mathematical Formulation. This system for sure, has created numerous IITians and others with excellent problem solving skills and sound analytical abilities, but unfortunately not many with the capability to Invent/Innovate.

Elevation of Computer Science as a Core Subject

Computer Science has been elevated to the level of a core subject with Programming introduced from Primary Level. The Students will learn the implementation of Algorithms through C / C++ from Std VI onwards.

The Stepping stone Advantage

Everything is taught under one roof. There is no necessity for Additional Academic Training of any sort than that of Stepping stone. Hence it will also be convenient for the Parents & Students alike.

Making The Students Experience The Joy of Learning

Our methodology is not focused on examination. We invoke students to cultivate knowledge and talent by the way of enjoying the studies.

Imparting Proficiency in Language & Social Science

Languages offered for Learning are Tamil, Hindi & Sanskrit. Efforts have been taken to make Social Science as interesting as possible, which is a crucial component of NTSE.
